RUSD AAC Program — Parent Contact Form Builder

Apps Script | Builds the Google Form automatically in your Drive | Rialto Unified School District

What this builds: A family-facing contact form for the public section of the RUSD AAC Hub. Families can submit questions, request a meeting, or ask to observe the classroom — without needing to know who to contact or what email address to use. You receive an email notification for every submission. An automatic confirmation is sent back to the family in their preferred language (English or Spanish).

After running the script: Complete the three manual steps below to set up the auto-response and notification, then embed the form in the Hub public section.

How to run this script

  1. Go to script.google.com and sign in with your RUSD Google account.
  2. Click New Project. Delete the default code.
  3. Copy the script below and paste it in.
  4. Click Run (triangle button) or press Ctrl+R.
  5. On first run: click Review permissions → choose your RUSD account → Allow.
  6. A popup will confirm the form was created and show its URL. Check your Google Drive.
  7. Complete the three manual steps listed below the script.

⚠️ Complete these three steps manually after running the script

  1. Set the confirmation message: Open the form → Settings (gear icon) → Presentation tab → Confirmation message. Paste the English + Spanish message from the preview below.
  2. Turn on email notifications: Responses tab → three-dot menu → Get email notifications for new responses. Every submission will immediately email [email protected].
  3. Link to a Google Sheet: Responses tab → Link to Sheets. Name the sheet "AAC Program — Family Contact Log." This creates a searchable record of every submission.
Auto-response note: Google Forms sends the confirmation message automatically to anyone who completes the form — but only if they have a Google account and the form is set to collect email addresses. The script sets setCollectEmail(true) so the family's email is collected. The confirmation message below (English + Spanish) is what they receive immediately after submitting. You still need to follow up personally based on the notification you receive.

Parent Contact & Meeting Request Form

This form appears in the public (no password) section of the RUSD AAC Hub. It collects the family's name, their child's school, preferred language, preferred contact method, reason for reaching out, and any message they want to share in advance.

function buildParentContactForm() {

  var form = FormApp.create('RUSD AAC Program — Contact Us / Request a Meeting');

  form.setDescription(
    'Questions about the AAC program? Want to schedule a meeting or observe your child’s classroom? ' +
    'Complete this form and the District AAC Lead SLP will follow up within two school days.

' +
    '¿Tiene preguntas sobre el programa de CAA? ¿Quiere programar una reunión u observar el salón de su hijo/a? ' +
    'Complete este formulario y el Coordinador de CAA del Distrito se comunicará con usted dentro de dos días escolares.'
  );

  form.setCollectEmail(true);   // Enables automatic confirmation email to family
  form.setAllowResponseEdits(false);
  form.setLimitOneResponsePerUser(false);  // Families may submit more than once

  var schools = [
    'Levi Bemis Elementary', 'J.C. Boyd Elementary', 'Merle S. Casey Elementary',
    'Sam V. Curtis Elementary', 'Helen L. Dollahan Elementary', 'George H. Dunn Elementary',
    'Dr. Edward Fitzgerald Elementary', 'Dr. Ernest Garcia Elementary',
    'Lida M. Henry Elementary', 'Elizabeth T. Hughbanks Elementary',
    'J.P. Kelley Elementary', 'Nancy R. Kordyak Elementary',
    'Lena M. Preston Elementary', 'A.H. Morgan Elementary',
    'Georgia F. Morris Elementary', 'W.A. Myers Elementary',
    'Samuel W. Simpson Elementary', 'W.J.C. Trapp Elementary',
    'Charlotte N. Werner Elementary', 'Warren H. Frisbie Middle School',
    'William G. Jehue Middle School', 'Ben F. Kolb Middle School',
    'Ethel Kucera Middle School', 'Rialto Middle School',
    'W.A. Carter High School', 'Dwight D. Eisenhower High School',
    'Dr. John Milor High School', 'Rialto High School',
    'Zupanic Virtual Academy', 'Adult Transition Program (ATP)',
    'I am not sure / My child is not yet enrolled'
  ];

  // ── SECTION 1: Who is reaching out ─────────────────────
  form.addSectionHeaderItem()
    .setTitle('About You  /  Sobre usted');

  form.addTextItem()
    .setTitle('Your name  /  Su nombre')
    .setRequired(true);

  form.addTextItem()
    .setTitle('Your child’s first name (or initials)  /  Nombre de su hijo/a (o iniciales)')
    .setHelpText('First name or initials only, please.  /  Solo el primer nombre o iniciales, por favor.')
    .setRequired(true);

  form.addListItem()
    .setTitle('Your child’s school  /  La escuela de su hijo/a')
    .setChoiceValues(schools)
    .setRequired(true);

  // ── SECTION 2: Language and contact preference ──────────
  form.addSectionHeaderItem()
    .setTitle('How to reach you  /  Cómo contactarle');

  form.addMultipleChoiceItem()
    .setTitle('Preferred language for our response  /  Idioma preferido para nuestra respuesta')
    .setChoiceValues([
      'English',
      'Spanish  /  Español',
      'English and Spanish  /  Inglés y español'
    ])
    .setRequired(true);

  form.addMultipleChoiceItem()
    .setTitle('Best way to reach you  /  Mejor manera de contactarle')
    .setChoiceValues([
      'Email (address collected above)',
      'Phone call  /  Llamada telefónica',
      'Either is fine  /  Cualquiera está bien'
    ])
    .setRequired(true);

  form.addTextItem()
    .setTitle('Best phone number (if you selected phone)  /  Número de teléfono (si seleccionó llamada)')
    .setHelpText('Leave blank if email is preferred.  /  Deje en blanco si prefiere correo electrónico.')
    .setRequired(false);

  form.addTextItem()
    .setTitle('Best time to reach you (if calling)  /  Mejor hora para llamarle')
    .setHelpText('Example: mornings before 9am, after 3pm  /  Ejemplo: mañanas antes de las 9am, después de las 3pm')
    .setRequired(false);

  // ── SECTION 3: Reason for contact ──────────────────────
  form.addSectionHeaderItem()
    .setTitle('What can we help you with?  /  ¿En qué podemos ayudarle?');

  form.addMultipleChoiceItem()
    .setTitle('I am reaching out because…  /  Me comunico porque…')
    .setChoiceValues([
      'I have a general question about the AAC program  /  Tengo una pregunta general sobre el programa de CAA',
      'I have a question specifically about my child  /  Tengo una pregunta específica sobre mi hijo/a',
      'I would like to schedule a meeting  /  Me gustaría programar una reunión',
      'I would like to observe my child’s classroom  /  Me gustaría observar el salón de mi hijo/a',
      'I have a concern I would like to discuss  /  Tengo una preocupación que quisiera comentar',
      'Something else  /  Otro motivo'
    ])
    .setRequired(true);

  form.addParagraphTextItem()
    .setTitle('Anything you’d like to share in advance  /  Algo que quisiera compartir de antemano')
    .setHelpText('Optional. This helps us prepare before we reach out to you.  /  Opcional. Esto nos ayuda a prepararnos antes de contactarle.')
    .setRequired(false);

  Logger.log('Parent Contact form created: ' + form.getPublishedUrl());
  Browser.msgBox(
    '✅ Parent Contact form created!

Check your Google Drive.

Form URL:
' + form.getPublishedUrl() +
    '

Next: complete the 3 manual steps in the builder page
(confirmation message, email notification, link to Sheet).'
  );
}

Confirmation message to paste into form settings (Settings → Presentation → Confirmation message)

If your matter is urgent, you can also email the District AAC Lead SLP directly at [email protected].

Si su asunto es urgente, también puede enviar un correo electrónico directamente al Coordinador de CAA del Distrito: [email protected].

Embedding the form in the Hub

In Google Sites: open the public Hub page → Insert → Embed → paste the published form URL. Google Sites will render the form inline. Alternatively, use a Button or Card link that opens the form in a new tab — this works better on mobile.

The form URL appears in the popup after you run the script, and is also available by opening the form and clicking Send → link icon.
